Hello, I'm Andrej

Professional Bartender and Cocktails Master

First of all let me introduce myself, I'm just an ordinary person whose
passion for bartending was born at a younger age, when I was only searching
for a summer job just to make some extra cash for myself, but not knowing
I would find something way better than just a summer job, i found my passion.
Beginnings
I began my journey at a local café bar in my town, with not a single drop of experience in me, I did not give up and went along the way without skipping any steps and carefully learning from the older more experience colleagues, the first two weeks I wasn't even paid for my work because I was new and unexperienced. Two months pass and next thing you know I'm catching up to my colleagues in experience. Third month go by and I get a praise from the owner of the bar for my fast learning, and love for the work.
Journey
My love for making cocktails was born when I just learned to make a simple cocktail with simple ingredients, that being the Blue Lagoon a few weeks in and compliments from the customers started flooding in we saw a huge increase in cocktail sales, which in return gave me motivation to learn new recipes for other cocktails, I did start off with simple ones but because I poured will and love into it, they came out perfect which the customers really loved. Having learned all the cocktails on our menu, I was already gaining and edge above the experience of my colleagues which surprisingly they embraced and supported me.
Advancement
As time went by, a new owner bought the café, he was more than fair and brought more life in it with new renovations, some menu changes and was rather humble and open to suggestions on improvements, as he knew that we understand what's missing and how the bar should operate. I took the chance and suggested that we buy more ingredients for a wider range of cocktails, and also some other improvements like changing the coffe for a better one, new coffe machine, new and better milk for latte art etc. He did accept the suggestions and next thing you know I have many new possible cocktail combinations and even room for some experiments. This was the key point to advancing my knowledge and skill to an expert level.
